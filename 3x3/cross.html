Cross

Making the cross of the cube is the first step in making the cube. It is the "C" part of CFOP. The goal of this first step is to make a cross of any color that's on the bottom layer, with edges that match its center. In this website, the bottom layer will be white meaning we will be making a white cross. For making the cross, it is mostly intuitive, meaning that it is better for one not to memorize algorithms, but instead, know how the cube works and make a cross. For beginners, the best way to start is to use the "Daisy Method" which is getting all the white edges on the top and then make a cross from there. To help you get started, here are some videos since there are no certain cases of algorithms to memorize.
