Permutation of the Last Layer (PLL)

PLL is the last step in solving a Rubik's Cube. It is the "P" in the method CFOP. The goal of the last step is to fully solve the last layer. The algorithms below uses yellow as the last layer, green as the front, red as the left, orange as the right, and blue on the back. What PLL does is swapping the corners, edges, or maybe even both. There are less algorithms to memorize than OLL since there are only 21 of them.

Edge Only

Ua Perm

M2' U M U2' M' U M2'

Ub Perm

M2' U' M U2' M' U' M2'

H Perm

M2' U M2' U2 M2' U M2'

Z Perm

M2' U' M2' U' M' U2 M2' U2 M' [U2]

Corners Only

Aa Perm

x (R' U R') D2 (R U' R') D2 R2 x'

Ab Perm

x R2' D2 (R U R') D2 (R U' R) x'

E Perm

x' (R U' R' D) (R U R' D') (R U R' D) (R U' R' D')

Swap One Set of Adjacent Corners

Ra Perm

(R U' R' U') (R U R D) (R' U' R D') (R' U2 R') [U']

Rb Perm

(R' U2 R U2') R' F (R U R' U') R' F' R2 [U']

F Perm

(R' U' F')(R U R' U')(R' F R2 U')(R' U' R U)(R' U R)

Ja Perm

(R' U L' U2) (R U' R' U2 R) L [U']

Jb Perm

(R U R' F') (R U R' U) R' F R2 U' R' [U']

T Perm

(R U R' U') (R' F R2 U') R' U' (R U R'F)

Swap Corners Diagonally

V Perm

(R' U R' U') y (R' F' R2 U') (R' U R' F) R F

Y Perm

F (R U' R' U') (R U R' F') (R U R' U') (R' F R F')

Na Perm

(R U R' U) (R U R' F') (R U R' U') (R' F R2 U') R' U2 (R U' R')

Nb Perm

(R' U R U') (R' F' U' F) (R U R' F) R' F' (R U' R)

G Perms (Double Cycles)

Ga Perm

R2 U (R' U R' U') (R U' R2) D U' (R' U R D') [U]

Gb Perm

(F' U' F) (R2 u R' U) (R U' R u') R2

Gc Perm

R2 U' (R U' R U) (R' U R2 D') (U R U' R') D [U']

Gd Perm

D' (R U R' U') D (R2 U' R U') (R' U R' U) R2 [U]
