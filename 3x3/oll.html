Orientation of the Last Layer (OLL)

2-Look OLL

2-look OLL is a two-step method to orientate the last layer. The first step is to make a cross or "orient the edges." Then, continue on the corners. 2-look OLL is easier to memorize because there are only 10 algorithms other than full OLL which has over 50 algoritihms. The downside is that in doing this, you will have more moves meaning that it will take more seconds to solve.

1st Look

Line

F (R U R' U') F'

Elbow

f (R U R' U') f'

Dot

[F (R U R' U') F'] [f (R U R' U') f']

2nd Look

Sune

R U R' U R U2 R'

Anti-Sune

R U2 R' U' R U' R'

H

F (R U R' U') (R U R' U') (R U R' U') F'

Pi

R U2 R2' U' R2 U' R2' U2' R

Headlights

R2 D (R' U2 R) D' (R' U2 R')

T

(r U R' U') r' (F R F')

Bowtie

F' (r U R' U') r' F R
