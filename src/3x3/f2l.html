First 2 Layers (F2L)

The First 2 Layers is the second step in solving the Rubik's Cube and it is the "F" in CFOP. This step is mainly the part of your journey where you really get to learn how the cube works. Again, this step is mainly intuitive. There are some cases which I can put on the site later that can help you solve F2L, but like I said, this is where you learn the cube. These videos really helped when I was beginning to solve a Rubik's cube.
