Cuber's Algo

Cuber's Algorithm gives you the algorithms to solve a Rubik's Cube. This site focuses more on the CFOP method which is the most famous method when solving a Rubik's cube. The method is basically solving the cube layer by layer. Some of the steps shows you to videos and some shows you cases for algorithms. This site also has a timer for you to quickly practice those algorithms you have learned. Enjoy!
